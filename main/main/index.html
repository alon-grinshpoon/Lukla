Intellectual Property

  • Intellectual property is commonly abbreviated as "IP".
  • IP is any work or invention that is the result of your creativity to which you own rights.
  • Intellectual property includes any algorithm or a design you create.
  • If a founder leaves without the IP being assigned to the other founder or an entity, then use of such IP created by the former founder may be problematic.
